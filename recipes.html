Recipes

Tasty recipes, easy steps, and delicious results!

  • 5-Minute Guacamole

    Ingredients: Avocados, lime juice, salt, garlic, and cilantro.

    Instructions:

    1. Mash avocados in a bowl.
    2. Add lime juice, salt, and minced garlic.
    3. Mix in chopped cilantro. Serve fresh!

  • Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

    Ingredients: Spaghetti, ground beef, onion, garlic, tomato sauce, Italian herbs.

    Instructions:

    1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
    2. In a pan, sauté onion and garlic, then add ground beef until browned.
    3. Stir in tomato sauce and Italian herbs. Simmer for 10 minutes.
    4. Serve sauce over spaghetti.

  • Chocolate Chip Cookies

    Ingredients: Butter, sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, flour, baking soda, salt, chocolate chips.

    Instructions:

    1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
    2. Cream butter and sugar, then mix in eggs and vanilla.
    3. Add flour, baking soda, and salt, then fold in chocolate chips.
    4. Scoop dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
